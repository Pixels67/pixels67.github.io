About::Me

I'm a programmer who enjoys making games from the ground up. I primarily work with C++, with experience in Unity and Rust as well. I'm also interested in graphics programming and pixel art.

My journey started with Construct 3, which I found quite limiting, and later I moved to Unity. Around that time I learned C++ through making games with Raylib, and now I'm developing PiXF, my own game engine project built with C++ and OpenGL.
